About us

The fundraising fashion gala was started by Nivea Debra in patnership with Delight Africa to raise funds for Sidon Children's home. Her love for Fashion and community development enabled her to start this project in a bid to give children from Sidon children's home something to smile about. The funds raised during the event are used in building better structures at the home. They also stand to benefit more with other opportunities that you share with them including bringing more patners on board for the betterment of their livelihoods. Our mission is to uplift the living standards of children in the home and to ensure we touch a heart.

First fundraising event

In patnership with designers from DelightAfrica and models from ND model academy, we were able to successfully launch our first fashion gala at Aden Valley Serviced Apartments. Several designers showcased their works and artists such as Makadem and Japolo performed.

event
May 23, 2021

Visited Sidon

They say home is where the heart is. We visited our home, Sidon where we spent the day with the kids. We were able to donate several clothes, foods and toys for the kids. We played, danced and sang various songs with the kids. It was such a memorable and pleasant day.

event
July, 2021

Cementing and Electricity

Using the money donated at the event, we launched our first project which was to cement the floors at the children's home. Before, the children had to lay their mattresses on the uncemented floor and sleep there because they did not have enough beds. this caused a lot of health respiratory illnesses. We are glad that we were able to help them cement the floors. Futhermore, we put up electricity so as to enable them to do tasks such as reading at their own comfort
